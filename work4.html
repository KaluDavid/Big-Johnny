Revival Church Mobile App

Client: Revival Church mobile app

My role: UI/UX Designer and Researcher

Project type: Mobile App Design

Deliverables: UX & UI Design UX Research, & UI components

About App:

Revival Church App is a mobile app created for direction chapel international Church to add to their digital experience. The app provides its users with the ability to watch past sermons on-demand, as well as live worship experience broadcasts.

The
Problem:

As a user of the DCI app, I have noticed that although it provides easy and convenient access to sermons, there is no way of connecting and interacting with other users — an element that would truly reinforce their global digital community

The
Solution:

As a result, I decided to create an opportunity where users can meet and communicate with other users, through a chat room.

Which include:

  • seamless book search
  • imely notifications
  • rating and review features
  • audio read mode
  • community channels
