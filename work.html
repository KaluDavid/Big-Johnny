iPlayer Mobile App

Full case study

Client: iPlay mobile app

My role: UI/UX Designer and Researcher

Project type: Mobile App Design

Deliverables: UX & UI Design UX Research, Style guide & UI components

Overview:

iPlayer is a versatile music player mobile app designed to address users' pain points, providing seamless access to both online and offline music. With a user-friendly interface and innovative features, iPlayer aims to enhance the music listening experience.

Why iPlayer:

iPlayer caters to the diverse preferences of users, offering offline features like local playlist access and recent plays, along with online features such as unlimited access to trending, latest, and suggested songs. It addresses the limitations of existing music players, providing a solution for users who want a blend of offline and online functionalities.

The Problem:

Existing music players often focus solely on offline features, restricting users to older songs.

Users Face Challenges Such As:

  • Difficult Registration Processes,
  • Poor UI, And

The Absence Of Features Like

  • Sharing Songs With Friends,
  • Background Play, And
  • Queue Management.

The Solution:

iPlayer introduces a holistic solution, allowing users to play and download songs both online and offline.

Key features:

  • Queueing System,
  • Downloading Music Lyrics,
  • Access To Other Music Sites, And
  • Integration With local And Online Videos.

It aims to revolutionize the music player experience by addressing user pain points and introducing exciting features.

Research Purpose:

Qualitative research involving 50+ participants aimed to understand user experiences, pain points, and expectations from music player apps. The target audience, primarily aged 18–30, expressed a desire for a music player that combines online and offline functionalities.

Research Method:

A combination of one-on-one user interviews and online questionnaires formed the qualitative research approach. This user-centered method ensured the efficient gathering of valuable insights to inform the design and development of iPlayer.

Designing iPlayer for User Satisfaction

Hybrid Experience Integration:

Online Features: Unlimited access to trending, latest, and suggested songs fulfills the needs of users who enjoy online playlists.

Offline Features: Local playlist access, downloaded songs, and recent plays cater to users who prefer offline music.
