Research Purpose:

Qualitative research involving 50+ participants aimed to understand user experiences, pain points, and expectations from music player apps. The target audience, primarily aged 18–30, expressed a desire for a music player that combines online and offline functionalities.

Research Method:

A combination of one-on-one user interviews and online questionnaires formed the qualitative research approach. This user-centered method ensured the efficient gathering of valuable insights to inform the design and development of iPlayer.

Designing iPlayer for User Satisfaction

Hybrid Experience Integration:

Online Features: Unlimited access to trending, latest, and suggested songs fulfills the needs of users who enjoy online playlists.

Offline Features: Local playlist access, downloaded songs, and recent plays cater to users who prefer offline music.

Innovative Features:

Queue Management:The introduction of a queueing feature addresses the user demand for better control over their music playback experience.
